 
Problem Statement: You need to scrape/crawl the forum [https://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/]. You will have to navigate to every post/news and collect the information for various posts as follows:



              ‘post_title': title of the thread under crawl,

              'post_id': unique identifier of the concerned post. To be scraped form the site,

              'post_url': url of the post under crawl,

              'publish_time’: time when the post was published,

              'fetch_time’: time when the post was fetched,

              'author': author of the post,
              
              'post_text': text content of the post,
              
              
              
              
              
             ------------- Note------
              Crawl it from terminal using ----   scrapy crawl headrun_spider.
